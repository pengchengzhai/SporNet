SporNet is a social networking app based on individual's sports preference. It origins from “Sport + Net”, which deliver a brand new sports social networking platform. Users are able to share similar interests by setting preferred sports and time. SporNet’s mission is to create the latest social platform that helps all sports lovers network, bond, and connect.
